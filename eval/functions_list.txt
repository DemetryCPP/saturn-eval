sin, cos - sinus and cosius
tg or tan - tangent

abs - absolute value of a number
exp - exponent
ln - logarithm with rig e
lg - logarithm with rig 10
sqrt - square root of number

sh or sinh - hyperbolic sinus
ch or cosh - hyperbolic cosinus
tgh or tanh - hyperbolic tangent

asin or arcsin - invert sinus
acos or arccos - invert cosinus
atg, arctg, atan or arctan - invert tangent
